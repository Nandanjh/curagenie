About Curagenie

Transforming healthcare access through technology and human expertise

Our Mission

At Curagenie, we're on a mission to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone through a seamless digital experience that combines the best of technology and human expertise.

Our Vision

We envision a world where finding the right doctor, booking appointments, and accessing hospital services is as easy as ordering food online—but with the trust and reliability that healthcare demands.

Our Approach

Unlike purely algorithmic solutions, Curagenie validates AI recommendations with real human insights, collected in-person by our team from clinics and hospitals, ensuring you get the best possible care.

How It Works

A seamless journey from symptoms to treatment

1

Symptom Assessment

Answer a few questions about your symptoms through our intuitive interface on mobile or web.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI analyzes your symptoms and suggests possible causes with remarkable accuracy.

3

Doctor Matching

Based on your condition, we recommend the best doctors, verified by real human reviews.

4

Appointment Booking

View available slots and book appointments that are confirmed by hospital staff in real-time.

5

Hospital Services

Check bed availability and other hospital services if admission is required for your treatment.
